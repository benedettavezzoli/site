Hello everyone!
Benedetta here.

I was born and raised in Brescia, Italy
and I really like it here.
Besides design, my hobbies include dance, music, photography and watching (binge watching...) movies and cartoons.

I am now an extremely determined, detail-oriented, and motivated UX Designer, always seeking new stimuli.

It all started with communication though, because, if effective, it is crucial not only for comprehending user requirements, behaviours and pain points, but also for gathering insights, proposing justifiable solutions and, ultimately, achieving set goals in every field. That’s why I earned a bachelor’s degree in ‘Communication, Media, and Advertising’ from IULM University.

I delved into the world of programming and UX Design as a self-taught individual and have never looked back. This dedication led me to acquire certification for the
‘Google UX Design Professional Certificate.’

To me, User Experience Design involves leveraging current situations, trends, technologies, and spatial arrangements of materials to create something fitting for the present while steering it towards a better direction, making
‍life easier for everyone!
